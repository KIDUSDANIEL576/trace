# Trace — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: July 19, 2026_

Trace is a drawing app for two people. Privacy is the product: your canvas is
visible to exactly one other person — your partner — and to no one else.

## What we store

- **Your email address** — used only to sign you in (one-time codes). No
  passwords, no marketing.
- **A display name** you choose — shown only to your partner.
- **Your drawings** (stroke data) and **photos you add** to draw on — stored in
  our database and private storage (Supabase, hosted in the EU,
  eu-central-1), readable only by the two members of your couple, enforced by
  row-level security.
- **A widget snapshot** — a rendered image of your latest canvas so your
  home-screen widget can display it. Invisible-ink strokes are never included.
- **A push notification token** (if you allow notifications) so your partner's
  drawings can reach you.
- **Purchase state** — whether your couple unlocked Trace Forever. Payments
  are processed by Apple/Google and RevenueCat; we never see card details.

## What we don't do

- No analytics SDKs, no ad networks, no trackers.
- No selling or sharing of data with third parties beyond the processors named
  above (Supabase, Expo push delivery, RevenueCat, Apple/Google).
- No AI training on your drawings. Your traces are yours.

## Deleting your data

Delete your account any time in the app (long-press the **trace** wordmark →
Delete account, or from the pairing screen). Deletion is immediate and
permanent: your login, name, and push token are erased. If you are the last
member of your couple, every drawing, photo, and snapshot goes with you. If
your partner remains, the shared canvases stay with them — they were made by
both of you.
